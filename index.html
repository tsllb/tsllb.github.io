    I received the B.E. degree from Beijing Jiaotong University, Beijing, China, in 2014, and is currently working towards the Ph.D. degree (supervised by Prof. Zhenjiang Miao) at Beijing Jiaotong University. In 2018, I was a visiting student (supervised by Prof. Song Wang) at the University of South Carolina, Columbia, supported by the China Scholarship Council (CSC). I'm working closely with Dr.Hongkai Yu. My current research interests include salient object detection and image classification.

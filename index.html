Shaoyue Song
    received the B.E. degree from Beijing Jiaotong University, Beijing, China, in 2014,
    and is currently working towards the Ph.D. degree (supervised by Prof. Zhenjiang Miao)
    at Beijing Jiaotong University.
    In 2018, she was a visiting student (supervised by Prof. Song Wang)
    at the University of South Carolina, Columbia,
    supported by the China Scholarship Council (CSC).
    She is working closely with Dr.Hongkai Yu
    Her current research interests include salient object detection and image classification.
